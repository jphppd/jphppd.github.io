Fields§

§last_update_timer: Option<Timer>

Time of the last polled value

§polled_values: PolledValues

Array holding the past boolean polled values of the DFC77 pin input at a “high” rate

§phase_detector: PhaseDetector

Detector of the phase of the signal, that is, detect the beginning of UTC seconds among the polled values

§last_peak_update: Option<Timer>

Time of the last detected peak of the correlation between the samples and the pattern, signaling a new bit

§signals: Signals

Array holding the decoded, 1Hz-bits of the DCF77 signal
